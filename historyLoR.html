LoR's history

The history of this game can essentially be summed up as one long stream of powerful card releases and sets that turned LoR's base simplistic game play loop into very pushed archetypes of resource heavy gameplans, faster and more consistent aggro strategies, and bigger and stronger midrange.

The most horrid and degenerate deck to this day was Azir Irelia. A deck that defied all known logic and came into the game at full power, immediately becoming a tier 0 deck that had the consistency and power level to often win on turn 5-7 decisively.
