Where LoR started

When Covid Locked us away

During the covid lockdown period of 2020-2021 there were many students like myself who felt the suffocating sense of confinement that came from spending every day within our homes, unable to interact with others and the world in person. Many of us turned to sources of entertainment that could stifle away that looming sense of dread and apprehension. For me this source of entertainment would come in the form of Riot Game's new foothold in the online card game genre. Legends of Runeterra released on April 29th, 2020. Although it did not pick up a lot of traction in the beginning the game would rise to become one of the market's best online card games that could compete with others that released during this time period. Yugioh Master Duel and Magic the Gathering Arena to name a few.

Before I knew it the first 3 months of the game would fly by and blend the monotone days of lockdown together. We would eventually reach Runeterra's first card set expansion since the initial release.
