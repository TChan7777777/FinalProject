Get to know me

Hey my name is Tobey Chan. For the last 3 years I have been an active player for the League of Legends online card game, Legends of Runeterra (LoR for short). I started playing LoR during the covid lockdown period of 2020-2021. While finishing up my junior year and heading into my senior year of high school, I discovered the game and immediately fell in love. I am a huge card game enthusiast and picked up a couple of other online card games before LoR. Legends of Runeterra however was simply the card game I stuck by and it ultimately became the game I was most confident in. Below are some interesting facts about me and my involvement with this game:

  • I have reached Masters rank in the game's ranked competitive ladder format a total of 5 times during the last 3 years
  • I have also been in the top 100 ranked Masters players on the game's ranked leaderboard for the NA server
  • My favorite playstyle in LoR and most card games in general is aggro/burn
  • I was eligible to play in multiple seasonal tournaments hosted on the game's client but was unable to due to priorities and responsibilities to school
  • I once dedicated 8 hours every day for 4 days straight to practice and climb to the highest point I could on the Masters leaderboard stopping at rank #60 on the NA server
