What is LoR

The Overview

Legends of Runeterra or LoR for short, is the League of Legends online card game published by Riot Games and is inspired by the collectible card game, Magic the Gathering. When developing the game, the devs had one huge goal in mind which was lowering the barrier of entry for LoR to compete against other games in its genre. The game, since its release back in April of the year 2020, has remained as one of the market's most free to play friendly online card games, and only has monetised through card skins and board cosmetics.

Like other collectible card games, players play one versus one to reduce their opponent's health to zero. For LoR players both start at 20 health.
